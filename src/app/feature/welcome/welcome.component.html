Profile

Dr. Kasi Reddy Mannem received his Veterinary Master Degree from the P V Narasimha Rao Veterinary University, Rajendranagar, Hyderabad. Following graduation, to further specialized his skills in small animal practice (pets).

By obtaining a large array of clinical experience working in high volume facilities providing the best quality care, Dr. Kasi Reddy Mannem has learned the critical components needed to operate a state-of-the-art veterinary hospital. He possesses the personal qualities-empathy, love of learning and its application, and kindness-that are needed to provide outstanding veterinary care. He is excellent at tackling the complexities that arise while treating animals suffering from multi-factorial disease processes.
